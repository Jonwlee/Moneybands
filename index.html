Microsoft Band Interaction

The Microsoft Band allows us to keep track of how active you are. With the built-in GPS, pedometer, gyroscope and heart rate sensor, we can determine how much you've ran.

Goals

You can push yourself based on what you want to see. You choose how much you want to run by the end of the week. If you don't meet you goal, you have to donate a certain amount of money to charity. You also choose the amount of money.

How It Works

See yourself run or see your donations to charity. You have to reach your goal by the end of the week or else money will be automatically donated to charity. The Microsoft Band is critical to the collection of data so that we can see whether you met your goal or not.
